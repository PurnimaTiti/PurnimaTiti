80s Shoes, Sneakers, Jelly flats

80s shoes for girls and teens were primarily jelly shoes, cutout flats, canvas flat sneakers in bright colors, high top sneakers (white or bold colors), classic Converse, Minnetonka moccasins, booties, and penny loafers. 80s shoes for women were white sneakers, canvas flats, bow flats, huarache sandals, basic pump heels, wedges, strappy evening sandals, and loafers. For the counter cultures (rockers, hip hop, pop) there were also Doc Martin boots, slouchy boots, moto boots, and spiked heels.
